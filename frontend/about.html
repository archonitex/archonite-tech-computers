About

Owned By: Francis Carrière

Hi, I'm Francis! Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been fascinated by how things work, particularly computers. I started out taking apart old machines and learning how to put them back together—sometimes with a few extra parts left over, but always with a sense of accomplishment! Over the years, that childhood curiosity turned into a full-blown passion for building, repairing, and optimizing computers.

Fast forward 20 years, and I've had the privilege of working in software development and IT services, helping businesses streamline their systems and solve complex problems. Along the way, I realized there was something missing: a personal connection to the people who could really benefit from my expertise. That's why I’ve decided to launch a side business, Archonite Tech & Computers.

I want to bring my experience and passion for technology to local families and small businesses. Whether it’s fixing a slow computer, setting up a new smart home device, or offering advice on how to make your tech work better for you, I’m here to help. I'm excited to offer my services to the eastern ontario community and help people enjoy the technology that makes their lives easier.
